Panckaes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 0,5 cup
Egg 2

Steps:

Step 1: Mix all ingredients in a bowl.

Step 2: Hit a pan and pour the batter.

Step 3: Cook until golden on both sides.

Source: Pasta website

Pasta

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredient Quantity
Pasta 200 g
Tomato sauce 1 cup
Cheese 50 g

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water.
  2. Heat the sauce in a pan.
  3. Mix pasta with sauce and add cheese.

Source: Jill Silverman

Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredient Quantity
Tomato 2
Cucumber 1
Olive oil 1 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Wash and cut the vegetables.
  2. Put them in a bowl.
  3. Add olive oil and mix well.

Source: The Organic Kitchen
